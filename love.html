I love you

Meri jaan ho yrr, mujhe bas dhyaan rakhna hai aapka isiliye itna sochti hu par abse itna nhi, jo seedha feel karungi voh karungi, jyada sochunga nhi.

Mera har baar I love you bolna aapko, special hai aapse jyada mere liye, mera commitment ho, pyaar ho kabhi man nhi bharega mera.

Bas merse na jab mujhe need ho aur itna distance ho voh tolerate nhi hota, lekin ab jaise bhi hai, mujhe bas aapka rehna hai. Bhale kabhi kabhi dur hi sahi.

Mai itni desperate hu na aapke touch ke liye, bas kahi pe bhi, cheek, arm, bas kahi toh aur jab milta nhi hai bilkul acha nhi lagta.

Bas yahi hai aur bas I love you,
